 <p><strong>Report Overview</strong></p>
 
 
 
 <p>Flashing cement is a specially formulated asbestos-free cement used for repairing leaks in shingles, metal or asphalt roofs, concrete, SBS or APP modified bitumen membranes flashings on walls, flues, chimneys, downspouts, skylight flashings, and cornices. Flashing cement is produced from refined asphalt and low aromatic petroleum solvents, along with non-asbestos fibers added for reinforcement. It is also used as a membrane flashing adhesive. It is slump-resistant and highly flexible for high slope and vertical applications. Flashing cement can be applied using a square notched trowel or standard V-notched trowel. It is applied on both the back of the flashing sheet and the substrate for better adhesion.</p>
