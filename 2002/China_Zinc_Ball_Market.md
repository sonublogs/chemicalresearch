In 2023, China produced approximately 420,000 metric tons of zinc balls, with 80% consumed domestically and 20% exported. The market benefited significantly from China&#39;s rapid growth in electric vehicle production, which led to a 30% increase in demand for high-purity zinc balls for battery anodes in 2023. Technological advancements have focused on improving the sphericity and size uniformity of zinc balls, with new manufacturing processes achieving less than 2% size variation, crucial for high-performance batteries. The market is seeing strong growth in nano-zinc balls, increasing at 15% annually, driven by their enhanced reactivity in chemical processes. In 2023, stricter wastewater treatment regulations led to a 20% increase in the use of zinc balls for heavy metal removal in industrial effluents. The industry faces challenges from zinc price volatility, with prices fluctuating by up to 25% in 2023. However, increased recycling efforts are helping to stabilize raw material costs. Emerging applications in 3D printing of zinc-based components are showing promise, growing at 25% <strong>CAGR</strong>, albeit from a small base of 2% market share.
