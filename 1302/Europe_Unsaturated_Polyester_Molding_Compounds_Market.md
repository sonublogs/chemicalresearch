<h1>Europe Unsaturated Polyester Molding Compounds Market, Outlook and Forecast 2025-2030</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:16px">Unsaturated Polyester Molding Compounds (UPE Molding Compounds) are thermoset materials composed of unsaturated polyester resins, reinforcing fibers, fillers, and curing agents. These compounds offer superior mechanical properties, including excellent surface finish, dimensional stability, and high resistance to corrosion, chemicals, and extreme temperatures. Due to their exceptional strength-to-weight ratio and moldability, UPE molding compounds are widely used across multiple industries, including automotive, construction, marine, electrical, and aerospace.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:16px">The Europe Unsaturated Polyester Molding Compounds market was valued at <strong>US$ 345.82 million in 2024</strong> and is projected to reach <strong>US$ 512.46 million by 2030</strong>, registering a <strong><strong>CAGR</strong> of 6.78% during 2024-2030</strong>. The market growth is primarily driven by increasing demand in the automotive and construction industries. With the <strong>automotive sector accounting for 46.8%</strong> of the market share, UPE molding compounds are becoming essential due to their lightweight nature and cost-effectiveness compared to metals.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:16px">Historical trends suggest a steady increase in demand for <strong>bulk molding compounds (BMC) and sheet molding compounds (SMC)</strong>, with SMC dominating the segment due to its enhanced durability and superior structural integrity. Germany remains the largest market in Europe, holding <strong>35.4% of the market share</strong>, followed by the <strong>United Kingdom, France, and Italy</strong>.</span></p>
