These formulations prevent polymer degradation, discoloration, and brittleness caused by prolonged exposure to sunlight. The demand for anti-UV masterbatches is significant in industries such as packaging, agriculture, construction, and automotive, where materials are exposed to outdoor environments. By embedding UV stabilizers within polymers, these masterbatches extend product lifespans and maintain aesthetic and structural integrity.</span></p>
