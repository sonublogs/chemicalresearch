These stabilizers are primarily used to enhance the durability and resistance of PVC products, particularly in outdoor and weather-exposed applications. Unlike traditional stabilizers that might contain lead (Pb), the focus of this market is on non-toxic, environmentally friendly alternatives. These stabilizers are designed to maintain the integrity of PVC products, improving their resistance to heat, weathering, and aging, all while meeting regulatory standards for environmental safety. The rise in demand for sustainable solutions is driving innovation in this sector, as manufacturers look to reduce the environmental footprint of PVC products.</span></p>
