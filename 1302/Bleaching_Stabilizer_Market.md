By minimizing the rapid degradation of hydrogen peroxide, these stabilizers contribute to obtaining high-quality bleached products in various industries, such as textiles, paper, and electronics.</span></p>
 
 
 
 <p><span style="font-size:16px">These stabilizers are typically made from a variety of chemical compounds, such as alkali-based stabilizers, dispersants, and inorganic stabilizers, and they are formulated to meet the specific needs of different applications.
