These capsules are an ideal choice for encapsulating various substances, especially for the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, and food industries. Pullulan hollow capsules are biodegradable, safe for human consumption, and known for their ability to maintain the integrity of sensitive ingredients, such as probiotics and herbal extracts, without compromising their effectiveness.</span></p>
