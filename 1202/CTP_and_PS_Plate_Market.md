<h1>CTP and PS Plate Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p><span style="font-size:16px">The CTP (Computer-to-Plate) and PS (Pre-Sensitized) Plate market refers to the global industry involved in the production, distribution, and application of plates used in offset printing. PS plates are aluminum-based and coated with photosensitive material, enabling high-quality image transfers for printing applications. CTP technology automates the plate imaging process, improving efficiency and reducing production costs. The two primary types of PS plates are negative and positive, each with specific uses in the printing industry.</span></p>
